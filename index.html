To You...

Hello Prerita,
By now you’ve probably realized it’s me, Paul, your Bhalu Kumar.
I didn’t call. I didn’t text. Not because I didn’t want to, but because sometimes love means having the strength to stay silent rather than disturb the peace of the person you care about the most. Today you turned twenty, a beautiful milestone in your life. You deserve days like that to be filled with laughter, celebration, and dreams that feel within reach. I never wanted my presence to cast even the smallest shadow over that happiness, so I waited until this evening. But there is one truth I cannot keep to myself. I miss you.

And I have loved you, truly, deeply, without pretense. The kind of love that doesn’t compete, doesn’t demand, and doesn’t hold you back. The kind that simply wishes to see you rise. One day you will achieve great things through your own strength and determination. One day you will stand tall and make your father proud of the incredible daughter he raised. And when that day comes, I may not be standing beside you, but believe me, I will be celebrating you with a full heart.

And when that day comes, I will celebrate you not for anything it gives me, but because you deserve every bit of that victory.

Even if life keeps us walking different paths, my prayers will still carry your name. Your victories will still bring me joy, and your struggles will still touch my heart.
Because loving you was never about keeping you close. It was about believing in the person you are destined to become.

So go forward without fear. Chase your dreams with everything you have. Become the woman the world has yet to witness. And when that day comes, the day your light shines so brightly that the world cannot ignore it, remember that somewhere, quietly and sincerely, someone who loved you will still be proud of you.
